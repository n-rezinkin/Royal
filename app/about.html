About Royal Keepers



Royal Keepers is an effective point of simultaneous contact with numerous contractors. Our digital platform is fully independent and focuses on the services provided for commercial and residential properties.


Our mission is bringing together customers and the most reliable contractors and help your project happen at its best.



Our base contains a huge range of UK companies that provide high-quality services for commercial and residential properties. We carefully shortlist contractors to offer you only the most experienced and reliable ones.



Currently, we cover the following service domains:

  • Design
  • Scaffolding
  • Engineering
  • Interior fit out
  • Exterior fit out


Also, we are currently developing a few domains to launch within the next three-four months. One of the demanded domains here includes property management and building management services.
